Mentally Unhealthy Days in Iowa Counties

This choropleth map displays the average number of mentally unhealthy days per month experienced by adults in each county across the state of Iowa. The data are visualized using a graduated color scheme, with lighter shades representing fewer unhealthy days and darker shades representing more.

Bivariate Map of Poor Mental and Physical Health Days in Iowa Counties

This bivariate choropleth map shows the average number of poor mental and physical health days reported by adults in Iowa counties. Each color represents a combination of both health indicators, with lighter shades indicating fewer unhealthy days and darker shades showing higher distress levels. The map highlights geographic patterns of health challenges, helping identify areas that may benefit from targeted public health support.

Isarithmic Map of Yearly Total Precipitation in Iowa

This isarithmic map shows annual precipitation across Iowa, using color gradients and contour lines to represent total rainfall in inches. Drier areas in the south and west appear in yellow and green, while wetter regions in the northeast are shaded in dark blue. The data comes from the Iowa Environmental Mesonet (IEM).

South American Banana Trade: Flows and Trade Balance

This map illustrates banana trade flows in South America. Orange indicates exporting countries, and blue indicates importers. Arrow thickness shows trade volume, while circle size represents total trade activity, highlighting Ecuador and Colombia as key exporters, primarily to Argentina and Chile.
